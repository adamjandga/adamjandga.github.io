How to Create an Invoice Given an RO

  1. First we have to go onto ccc and type in the RO for the vehicle we are looking for
  2. Double click on the file, navigate to "Print", select "Estimate to Repair", then save as a pdf under the "r0#".pdf
          
  3. Navigate to adasthink, click on "New Report", upload the "ro#".pdf that you downloaded from ccc, copy the vin
          
  4. Now navigate to alldata, click "Create New Document" select new vehicle, paste in the vin from adasthink to add vehicle, then add the shop
  5. After inputting this data, you click apply, this next page is where you add in all the information
  6. Select "Custom Labor", under job description paste the job from adasthink
          
  7. The prices for the jobs are:
  8. Lane Change $400
  9. Seat Belt $175
  10. Blind Spot $600
  11. Steering Angle $289
  12. Front Radar $500
  13. Surrounded View Monitor $500
  14. Parking Sensors $375
  15. Seat Weight $289
  16. Once the price has been inputted, add "Note" for the job description
  17. Add "ADAS" in the top right under "Shop Note"
  18. Add the "RO#" in the "Service Tag" box
  19. Click on "Estimate" on the top, swithing your invoice out of "Draft"
  20. Make sure everything is inputted properly, then create RO and "Authorize"
  21. Now the invoice in completed, you can download and email to the estimator
